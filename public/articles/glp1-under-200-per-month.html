How to Get GLP-1 Medications for Under $200/Month

Updated April 20, 2026

In April 2026, four FDA-approved GLP-1 medications are available for under $200 per month at self-pay rates. That's a dramatic shift from even 18 months ago, when the cheapest cash price was closer to $500. If you're paying out of pocket and your target is to stay under $200, here are your options ranked from cheapest to most expensive — with the fine print that matters for each.

1. Foundayo: $149/mo Starting

The newest and cheapest GLP-1 at its entry price. Daily pill, no food restrictions. Available at $149/month for the starting dose through LillyDirect or Amazon Pharmacy. Climbs to $299–$349/mo at maintenance doses.

Gotcha: Only the starter dose is sub-$200. If you end up on a maintenance dose, the monthly price doubles. For ongoing under-$200 pricing, Foundayo doesn't hold.

2. Wegovy Pill: $149/mo at Every Dose

The strongest under-$200 option because TrumpRx prices it flat at $149/month across every dose tier. Whether you're on the starter or the maximum dose, the price is $149. Works out to $1,788/year for indefinite use.

Gotcha: Must be taken daily on an empty stomach, no food or drink other than 4oz water for 30 minutes after. If the morning fasting ritual is a non-starter for you, Foundayo doesn't have the restriction.

3. Ozempic: $199/mo Starting Dose

TrumpRx and GoodRx both price Ozempic at $199/mo for the 0.25–0.5mg starting dose. Once you escalate to 1mg (month 3–4) or 2mg (month 5+), the price rises to $349 on GoodRx and $350 on TrumpRx.

Gotcha: Only the first 2–3 months stay under $200. Not a long-term path unless your doctor keeps you at a very low dose.

4. Wegovy Injection: $199/mo at Every Dose

TrumpRx prices Wegovy injection at a flat $199/month across every dose, same structure as Wegovy pill. GoodRx has $199 intro pricing for the first 2 fills, then rises to $349+.

Gotcha: The TrumpRx flat rate is the stable version; the GoodRx intro is time-limited.

Which Options Stay Under $200 at Every Dose?

Only two medications stay under $200/month through maintenance:

  • Wegovy Pill on TrumpRx — $149/mo flat, every dose.
  • Wegovy Injection on TrumpRx — $199/mo flat, every dose.

If you need budget certainty over a 12-month horizon, these are the only two paths. Ozempic and Foundayo both rise above $200 as your dose goes up.

The $25/Month Path: Commercial Insurance + Savings Card

If you have commercial insurance that covers any GLP-1 (Ozempic, Wegovy, Mounjaro, Zepbound, or Foundayo), the manufacturer savings cards drop your copay to $25/month. That's under $200 by a wide margin. The catch:

  • Only works with commercial insurance (employer plans, marketplace plans).
  • Does not work with Medicare, Medicaid, Tricare, or if you're uninsured.
  • The plan must actually cover the drug. "Non-covered" means the savings card does much less (Lilly's "up to $499/$650 off" track, Novo's card does nothing).

If you qualify, this is the cheapest path. Full stop.

The Free Path: Patient Assistance Programs

If your household income is at or below approximately 400% of the federal poverty level (~$62,400/yr for a single person in 2026), you may qualify for:

  • NovoCare Patient Assistance Program — provides Wegovy (injection or pill) or Ozempic at no cost.
  • Lilly Cares — provides Zepbound at no cost. Foundayo is expected to be added within 2026. Mounjaro is not available through Lilly Cares.

Application takes 4–8 weeks. While waiting, self-pay via TrumpRx is a reasonable bridge.

What's NOT Under $200 (Even Discounted)

  • Mounjaro: Cheapest path is TrumpRx at $350/mo. No sub-$200 option exists.
  • Zepbound: Cheapest path is TrumpRx or LillyDirect starter at $299/mo. Close but no.

Both are tirzepatide. If your doctor has you on tirzepatide specifically and you need under $200, the alternatives are: (a) ask about Foundayo, which is also tirzepatide-class, (b) verify you have commercial insurance and a savings card drops you to $25, or (c) apply to Lilly Cares for Zepbound specifically.

Decision Guide

  1. Want the absolute cheapest FDA-approved GLP-1? Foundayo starter or Wegovy pill. Both $149/mo.
  2. Want sub-$200 pricing that holds at every dose? Wegovy pill ($149/mo TrumpRx) or Wegovy injection ($199/mo TrumpRx).
  3. Have commercial insurance? Savings card drops any GLP-1 to $25/mo.
  4. Low income + no insurance? NovoCare or Lilly Cares PAP — free.
  5. Need Mounjaro or Zepbound specifically? They don't fit under $200 on cash. Explore insurance or PAP paths.
