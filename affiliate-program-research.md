## PART 4: FTC Compliance & Disclosure Requirements

### Regulatory Framework

**Governing Authority:** Federal Trade Commission (FTC)  
**Key Regulation:** 16 CFR Part 255 (Endorsement Guides)  
**Applies To:** All affiliate links on glp1costfinder.com

---

### What Constitutes a Material Connection (Requires Disclosure)

You MUST disclose if:
- You receive a commission on referral links
- You receive products/services for free or at significant discount in exchange for reviews
- You're compensated in any way (including store credit, contest entries, paid trips)

**For glp1costfinder specifically:** Any affiliate link on your site has a material connection and requires clear disclosure.

---

### Required Disclosure Language

#### Placement (CRITICAL)

**Where:** Disclosure MUST appear:
- **Before any affiliate link** (not at bottom of page)
- **Near the link itself** on the same page section
- **In the first 25–30% of content** where readers naturally look
- **Large enough to be easily noticeable** — not tiny print

**What the FTC considers compliant:**
- "This link is an affiliate link. We earn a commission if you purchase through this link."
- "We may earn a commission from [Company Name] if you use our link to make a purchase."
- "Affiliate disclosure: We are a participant in the [Company] affiliate program and may earn a commission from your purchase."

---

#### Formatting Examples

**Homepage (Comparison List):**
```
IMPORTANT: glp1costfinder.com participates in affiliate programs with Ro, Hims, and other providers listed below. 
We earn a commission if you use our links to purchase or sign up. This does not affect the price you pay — 
you receive the same rates whether you use our link or go directly. Our recommendations are based on independent 
research and customer reviews, not affiliate relationships.
```

**Article with Affiliate Links:**
```
** Affiliate Disclosure: We may earn a commission if you purchase through the links below. 
Our analysis is independent and unbiased regardless of affiliate status. **
```

**Link Itself (if using button/call-to-action):**
```
[Visit Hims] (Affiliate Link — We Earn Commission)
```

---

### Health-Specific FTC Considerations

**For GLP-1 Cost Finder specifically:**

1. **Scientific Substantiation:** Any health claims (e.g., "GLP-1 drugs are effective for weight loss") require competent and reliable scientific evidence (typically clinical trials).

2. **Disclaimer on Comparisons:** If comparing effectiveness/cost of different GLP-1s, note:
   - "We compare pricing and availability; consult your doctor about medical suitability"
   - Avoid claims like "Wegovy is better than Ozempic" without clinical backing

3. **Patient Assistance Separation:** NovoCare and TrumpRx are patient assistance — make clear these are NOT affiliate programs and you don't earn commission:
   ```
   NovoCare is a free patient assistance program (we don't earn commission for referrals).
   ```

4. **Off-Label Use:** GLP-1s are FDA-approved for diabetes/weight loss. Avoid promoting them for other uses.

## PART 8: Risk Mitigation

### Regulatory Risk
- **Action:** Keep all FTC disclosures current; audit quarterly for compliance
- **Contingency:** Have legal review of disclosures before launch
- **Monitoring:** Subscribe to FTC endpoint updates on affiliate/endorsement rules

### Affiliate Program Discontinuation
- **Risk:** Programs may close or change terms without notice
- **Mitigation:** Diversify across at least 4–5 programs; don't depend on single program for >30% revenue
- **Action:** Monitor program terms monthly; have fallback links ready

### Low Conversion Rates
- **If <5% conversion:** Revise link placement, messaging, or audience targeting
- **If >30% conversion:** Optimize and scale; may indicate undermonetized page
- **Benchmark:** Industry average for comparison sites is 8–15%

### Brand Association Risk
- **Consider:** Some telehealth providers may face regulatory scrutiny
- **Mitigation:** Disclose clearly; recommend users consult their doctor; avoid medical claims
- **Contingency:** Be prepared to remove programs if regulatory issues arise
